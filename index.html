Our Journey

Crafting Excellence Since 2007

From a visionary beginning in Melvisharam to becoming one of South India's most trusted construction brands, SS BUILDERS MVS has been transforming landscapes and building legacies for over 17 years.

Our commitment to quality engineering, transparent processes, and innovative design has earned us the trust of hundreds of families and businesses across nine cities.

2007

The Foundation

SS BUILDERS MVS was founded in Melvisharam with a vision to bring premium construction quality to every home and business in the region.

2010

First 50 Projects

Completed our first 50 residential projects, establishing a reputation for quality craftsmanship and on-time delivery across Ranipet district.

2014

Commercial Expansion

Expanded into commercial construction and luxury villa projects. Opened service coverage to Vellore, Arcot, and Walajah.

2018

Interior & Exterior Design

Launched our dedicated interior and exterior design division, offering complete turnkey solutions from foundation to furnishing.

2021

Chennai & Bengaluru

Extended our premium services to Chennai and Bengaluru, serving the growing demand for luxury construction in metropolitan cities.

Today

60+ Projects & Growing

With over 70 successful projects, 100+ happy clients, and 9 cities served, SS BUILDERS MVS continues to set new standards in premium construction.
